Another WPA Key Generator

There are a million WPA key generators out there. However, given the number of devices we have that use wireless connections, I wanted to make a few improvements to help in situations where you cannot simply copy-paste the key:

  1. Remove characters that may appear ambiguous like "l" and "1", "0" and "O", etc.
  2. Group sets of similar characters together. I figure there are four groups of similar characters: upper case letters, lower case letters, numbers, and symbols. This makes it easier for smartphone users to enter a key.

Short disclaimer: Key generation takes place completely your browser, with no data transmitted back-and-forth to a web server. In other words, there should be no security concerns.
